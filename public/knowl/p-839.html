You can embed interactive demonstrations created in Wolfram's Computable Document Format so that they will be played with the Wolfram CDF Player™. Once you create and save a demonstration, you want to determine the UUID that is the identifier of your demonstration. For example, Figure 15.7 is identified by 9fa2acff-c809-4b7f-a73b-c59ace36affc. This identifier is enough to create the PreTeXt to embed the demonstration. See reference.wolfram.com/language/howto/DeployInteractiveContentInTheWolframCloud.html for information about creating your demonstration.
