Checkpoint 13.13. Changing Secant Lines.

When discussing the derivative as a limit, we think of the point of tangency as being fixed (the green point in 13.12) and the “other” point defining the secant line as changing (the red point in 13.12). Switch it up! Fix a large value of \(h\) (positive or negative) and then change the point of tangency (the green point). Discuss what you observe.
